About Us

Creating Agripreneurs, Not Just Farm Labourers

Simini Agribusiness Academy is a practical agricultural training institution based on a working farm in rural South Africa. We exist to curb youth unemployment and address the critical lack of business skills in agriculture.

We bridge the gap by combining practical farming experience with business training and market access. Our students learn to produce, manage, and sell because agriculture is about far more than planting. They leave as agripreneurs—innovative, independent, and ready to start their own agribusinesses.
